I'm Sorry

I haven't been who I should have been for you. There were moments you needed me close, and I was distant — emotionally, physically, in the ways that matter most. I let myself become out of reach, and I didn't communicate the way I should have. I let things go unsaid that deserved to be spoken, and let silence stand in where honesty belonged.

I hurt someone I love deeply, and that is not a small thing to me. I'm not writing this looking for excuses, because there aren't any that matter more than the simple truth: I could have shown up better, and I didn't. I take responsibility for that, fully and without conditions.

I promise to work on being more present — not just in the big moments, but in the ordinary, quiet ones too, because that's where love actually lives. You deserve consistency. You deserve honesty. You deserve patience, and a love that doesn't waver when things get difficult. You deserve someone who chooses you on the easy days and the hard ones alike.

So I'm asking, with everything I have, for another chance to be that for you. And whatever you decide — I will respect it completely. This is your choice to make, in your own time, and I won't make it harder than it already is.

With love, always.
